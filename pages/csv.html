File format for CSV uploads

In general, try not to upload CSVs of more than 500 items at a time. Larger files are unsupported and may fail.

CSV upload is incremental. If you upload the same rows twice, you will have duplicates.

CSV upload doesn't support all columns that normal addition does. This is a great way to quickly get a list of items into the system.

Column ordering
Music:
Artist, Title, Year Released, Note, Media, URL, QTY
